sur/la/route: Contents

Life is a dream, a sensorial experience. The goal is to focus on the moment since you can't stop time from running. Store an infinite numbers of pictures that when scrolled together can turn into a movie. Here is what I learned and kept so far. A fictional biography where I bump into living beings, places, colours, forms and patterns of various kind. When I see that the repetition is too regular, too obvious, I move somewhere else and start again from the scratch, following the same principle. I am trying to stick all those pictures on an imaginary wall and I want to do it in a certain order but I haven't found one yet. Here is about the last years, as far as I can recall. The rest is gone for the moment but hopefully it will come back in the future.
