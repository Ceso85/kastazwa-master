Time to fill this space with something new, with something that comes up daily from the never-ending changing of things, of colours, of moods, of impressions.
Time to write things down before the memories will vanish, to take notes of names, places, feelings.
The urge to leave a trace, the urge to follow a melody without knowing where it is going.
The urge to fill black with with white, the scream that bounces inside my mind. The lack of rules, living in a world where disorder prevails, a world of no control, of things far from being good.
The eternal search of something we can not find, the only thing that keeps the human alive.
